Admissions & Enrollment

Ozone High School welcomes students who are committed to academic excellence, discipline, and personal growth.
Our admissions process is designed to ensure that every student is prepared to succeed.

Requirements

Applicants are required to submit previous academic records, complete the official admission form, and meet the school's academic requirements.

Important Dates

Fall enrollment begins in August. Spring enrollment begins in January.
